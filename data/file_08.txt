Run notes - kept on phone, occasional updates

Neighborhood loop (Sunbury Rd north past the elementary school):
  3.0 mi if you turn around at the cemetery gate
  3.4 mi if you go to the small park and back
  3.7 mi if you push out to the strip mall corner
  Watch for the loose dog on Country Squire Dr around 7am
  Sidewalk is uneven on the south stretch, prefer the east side after rain

Alum Creek Trail:
  Park at the Lewis Center entrance, free lot
  Out and back to the wooden bridge = 4.0 mi
  Out and back to the next bend past the bridge = 5.2 mi
  Long run target: out to the 3 mi marker, back, 6.0 mi
  Morning light through the trees is the best part
  Trail closes for paving end of August every year, check before going

Things to remember:
  Bring extra water on Alum Creek if temp above 70
  Reflective vest before 6:30 in winter
  Phone in armband not waistband on long runs (rubs)
  Lemon ginger gel for anything over 5 mi
